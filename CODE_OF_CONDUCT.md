# Code of conduct

## Expected conduct

Participants must communicate respectfully, critique ideas rather than people,
protect private research data, obtain consent for human contributions, and
describe results honestly, including negative and inconclusive findings.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, non-consensual publication, and
fabrication or concealment of evidence are not acceptable.

## Scope and enforcement

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews,
and project events. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner
or through GitHub's abuse-reporting tools. Maintainers may edit or remove
content and restrict participation when necessary to protect contributors and
research participants.
